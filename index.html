About Us

The CitySeer team was founded in 2019 in a small classroom in New York City, NY. Forged by a vision to provide travelers the ultimate United States city research tool- travelers discover what this varied country has to offer. Each city is unique, and the team has worked hard to gather all the data in one, easy to use application.
